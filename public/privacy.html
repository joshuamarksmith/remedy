Remedy Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Summary

Remedy does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter stays on your device.

Data Storage

All data — including drink logs, body weight, biological sex, bedtime, and session history — is stored locally on your device using your browser's or app's local storage. No data is sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.

No Analytics or Tracking

Remedy does not use analytics services, advertising SDKs, crash reporters, or any form of tracking. We do not collect usage data, device identifiers, or IP addresses.

No Account Required

Remedy does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide an email address.

Health Information

Remedy is not a medical device. The BAC estimates and sleep impact calculations are approximate, for informational purposes only, and should never be used to make safety-critical decisions such as whether it is safe to drive.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from Settings > Reset. Uninstalling the app also removes all stored data.

Children

Remedy is intended for adults of legal drinking age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 17.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Open an issue on our GitHub repository.
